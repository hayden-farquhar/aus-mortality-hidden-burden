INTERPRETATION:

Hypertension (I10-I15) has a MUR of ~8 in 2023 data, meaning
hypertension is mentioned on death certificates ~8 times more
often as a contributing cause than as the underlying cause.

Despite increasing prescribing of antihypertensive medications,
hypertension-attributed mortality (as underlying cause) has been
increasing. This is consistent with:
  (a) Population ageing increasing the absolute number of deaths
  (b) Changes in certification practices
  (c) The paradox that treatment may shift deaths from acute
      hypertensive crises (underlying cause) to chronic conditions
      where hypertension is a contributor (increasing MUR)
